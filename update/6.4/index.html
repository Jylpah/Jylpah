6.4 Update

Update 6.4

This page list statistics of battles fought during 6.4. All the stats (WR, damage, battles, etc.) refer to stats during the update, not cumululative/total/lifetime stats unless otherwise mentioned.

Battles per Player

The graph shows the number of battles fought per player during the update 6.4. On average, players played 327 battles. Median was 184 and the largest number of battles recorded by a single player (in the dataset) during the update 6.4 was 77459 battles. The following tables shows battle counts by N% most inactive players during the update.
