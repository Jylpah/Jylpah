Career stats

This page shows statistics of players’ battles fought during their Blitz “career” until the end of update 6.7.

Battles per Player

Number of career/cumulative battles per player in the end of the update 6.7.

An average player has played 9727 battles by the end of update 6.7. Median battle count was 5668 and the largest number of battles recorded by a single player (in the dataset) was 179k battles.
