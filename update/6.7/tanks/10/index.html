The graph shows tanks with the highest Relative WR - that is the average of players’ WR in the tank compared to the players’ WR with tier other same tier tanks. Relative WR is a measure of the performance of the tank.

Show details
  • All the statistics are about battles fought during the update 6.7 only.
  • The impact of “Stock-grind battles” is reduced based on tier-specific requirement for total battles at the end of the update. Only players with more than 5-30 (depending on the tier) battles in a tank are included
  • Only players with 10-60 battles at the tier (tier depended) during the update are included.
  • Average WR is the players’s average WR in the tank
  • Player WR is the average WR of the players of the tank in all the other same tier tanks. Since different tiers have different difficulty, it is more meaningful to compare players’ performance in the same tier tanks.
