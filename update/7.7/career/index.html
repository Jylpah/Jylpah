7.7 Career stats

Career stats

This page shows statistics of players' battles fought during their Blitz “career” until the end of update 7.7.

Battles per Player

Number of career/cumulative battles per player in the end of the update 7.7.

An average player has played 11807 battles by the end of update 7.7. Median battle count was 6781 and the largest number of battles recorded by a single player (in the dataset) was 206k battles. The following tables shows battle counts by N% most inactive players during the update (i.e. “90% most inactive” == “10% most active”).
