6.0 Update

Update 6.0

This page list statistics of battles fought during 6.0. All the stats (WR, damage, battles, etc.) refer to stats during the update, not cumululative/total/lifetime stats unless otherwise mentioned. In total, there were 85.2M battles recorded by 205k players.

Battles per Player

The graph shows the number of battles fought per player during the update 6.0. On average, players played 416 battles. Median was 257 and the largest number of battles recorded by a single player (in the dataset) during the update 6.0 was 34.8k battles. The following tables shows battle counts by N% most inactive players during the update.
