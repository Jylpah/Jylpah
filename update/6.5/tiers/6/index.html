6.5 Tier VI Tier stats

Tier VI stats

This page list statistics of battles fought at tier VI during update 6.5.

Battles per Player

Number of battles fought per player during the update 6.5.

On average, players played 52 battles. Median was 20 and the largest number of battles recorded by a single player (in the dataset) during the update 6.5 was 9 120 battles. The following tables shows battle counts by N% most inactive players during the update.

Battles per Player table

The table below shows the (max) number of battles played during 6.5 by the X% most inactive players. A reading tip: 10% most active players played more than the 90% most inactive players.
