Career stats

This page shows statistics of players’ battles fought during their Blitz “career” until the end of update 6.1.

Battles per Player

Number of total battles per player in the end of the update 6.1. An average player has played 5481 battles by the end of update 6.1. Median battle count was 2956 and the largest number of battles recorded by a single player (in the dataset) was 154977 battles.
