Update 6.9

This page list statistics of battles fought during 6.9. All the stats (WR, damage, battles, etc.) refer to stats during the update, not cumululative/total/lifetime stats unless otherwise mentioned.

The graph shows the number of battles fought per player during the update 6.9. On average, players played 383 battles.
