Show details
  • All the statistics are about battles fought during the update 6.9 only.
  • Only players with more than 30 battles in a tank during the update and at least total 60 in the end of the update are included to eliminate the impact of the first battles in the tank (with sub-100% crew / modules).
  • Average WR is the players’s average WR in the tank
  • Player WR is the average WR of the players of the tank in all the other same tier tanks. Since different tiers have different difficulty, it is more meaningful to compare players’ performance in the same tier tanks.
