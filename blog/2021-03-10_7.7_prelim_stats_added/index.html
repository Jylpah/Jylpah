Update 7.7 preliminary stats added

Preliminary stats for update 7.7 have been added.

  • In total, there has been 115M battles by 465k players recorded in the database during the update 7.7 so far. Please note, these are note all the WoT Blitz players, just those tracked by BlitzAnalysiz[].

  • Update 7.7 brought new Czechoslovakian tanks and the “mighty” TVP T 50/51 tops the highest average damage tank list. But is it any good?

  • The new event tank Magnus took the 2nd spot on the best performing tanks list while the old, reliable Object 252U took the lead. Other tanks in the TOP 5 are familiar names too: T28 Concept, Annihilator and AT 15A. Chimera (8th) is the only medium tank that gets into TOP 10.

Czechoslovakian tanks

  • Yes, the Czechoslovakian tanks. They seem to be “meh” at the best. The data is still preliminary so let’s see if the final stats will change the picture.

Tier VI Škoda T 25

The tier VI Škoda T 25 appears on the BOTTOM 20 tanks list and even good players seem to struggle with it. So it seems it was not only myself thinking it sucks.

Tier VII Konštrukta T-34/100

I personally felt the tier VII Konštrukta T-34/100 was pretty decent, but still it seems to perform below average - even for the skilled players.

Tier VIII TVP VTU Koncept

The tier VIII TVP VTU Koncept follows the pattern. It performs well below average and sits between known underdogs Panther II and FCM 50 t.

Tier IX Škoda T 50

The tier IX Škoda T 50 performs slightly better, but still just average. I personally disliked also this one. It is slow, it has poor armor and its playstyle is boring.

Tier X TVP T 50/51

One could expect that the tier X TVP T 50/51 would be a culmination of excitement after all the miserable grind through the mediocre tanks on the line, but it does not seem to perform well at all. Not even for 55%+ players. I know some well-known YouTubers like it and it might be that it performs well only for Super unicums… Or then it is just exciting to play since based on data it performs below average even for players with 75%+ WR at tier X (I checked).

If you constantly see TVP T 50/51s to perform superb on the battlefield, that is simply because it is being played by very-very good players on average at this point. The rest of us will soon grind it so the true (non-)performance of the TVP will become apparent.
