Blitzanalysiz.com launched

2020-05-07

Hy!

BlitzAnalysiz.com is a site about in-depth game statistics of Wargaming’s World of Tanks Blitz. You won’t find individial player statistics here - for those, please check awesome Blitzstars.com. Instead, the the site is about analysis of the player population and tank performance statistics.

The statistics shown at the site are based on maybe the largest collection of Blitz player stats outside Wargaming’s servers containing over 100 million individual tank statistics from more than 600,000 players from EU, RU, Asia and North American servers.

BlitzAnalysiz() is build by Jylpah@EU. You can reach out me via email (Jylpah@gmail.com), Discord (Jylpah#4662). I am also active at WoT Blitz EU forums.
